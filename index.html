1. Introduction

Deep Dash is a mobile game developed by an independent creator. This privacy policy explains how user data is handled within the app. Deep Dash does not collect or store personal information directly. However, third-party services integrated into the app may process data to enable core features such as advertising and in-app purchases.

2. Data Collection

Deep Dash itself does not collect, store, or share any personal data. However, the following third-party services may collect data automatically:

  • Unity Ads: Used to display advertisements. May collect device information, ad engagement metrics, and identifiers to serve relevant ads.

These services operate independently and are governed by their own privacy policies.
