# Deep Dash Privacy Policy ## 1. Introduction Deep Dash is a mobile game developed by an independent creator. This privacy policy outlines how user data is handled within the app. Deep Dash does not collect or store personal information directly. However, third-party services integrated into the app may process data to enable core features such as in-app purchases. ## 2. Data Collection Deep Dash itself does not collect, store, or share any personal data. However, the following third-party services may collect data automatically to support billing functionality: - **Google Play Billing** (Android): Handles in-app purchases and may process transaction history, device identifiers, and account details. - **Apple In-App Purchases** (iOS): Facilitates purchases and may process similar data in accordance with Apple’s privacy practices. - **Unity IAP**: Used to manage cross-platform purchase flows. May interact with platform-specific billing services and process limited device data. These services operate independently and are governed by their own privacy policies. ## 3. Third-Party Services | Service | Purpose | Privacy Policy Link | |------------------------|---------------------|----------------------| | Google Play Billing | Android purchases | [Google Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy) | | Apple In-App Purchases | iOS purchases | [Apple Privacy Policy](https://www.apple.com/legal/privacy/) | | Unity IAP | Purchase management | [Unity Privacy Policy](https://unity.com/legal/privacy-policy) | ## 4. How Data Is Used Data collected by third-party services is used to: - Process and verify in-app purchases - Ensure secure transactions - Improve platform integration and user experience Deep Dash does not access or store this data. ## 5. Children’s Privacy Deep Dash is not intended for children under the age of 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided personal information through third-party services, please contact those providers directly. ## 6.
